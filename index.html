Clinical Disclaimer: This tool is for research and educational purposes only. Predictions use a Logistic Regression model trained on SEER (n=74,871) and externally validated on an independent Egyptian cohort (n=161, C-index=0.81) and TCGA (n=275, C-index=0.61). Inputs: age, FIGO stage, histology, grade, surgery, radiation, chemotherapy. ECOG performance status and pelvic pain — independent prognostic factors (Cox AHR 2.215 and 2.339) — are not model inputs. All clinical decisions must be made by qualified healthcare professionals. This output does not constitute medical advice.

Reference: Dweep M et al. Prognostic Factors and Externally Validated Survival Prediction Models for Cervical Cancer in Egypt: A Single-Centre Cohort Study. Ain Shams University, 2015–2021.
